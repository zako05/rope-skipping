Welcom to the website Crazy Jump

We are a bunch of people who jump rope. We want to expand rope skipping to Slovakia. Therefore we organize courses for children, youths and adults. We also organize various workshops, presentations and performances across the country.

Rope Skipping

Rope skipping is for everybody who loves sport. It is a fun, playful sport that develops creativity, physical fitness, body coordination, teamwork and rhythm. It contains elements of art, aerobics, dance, gymnastics, hip hop and breakdance. It boots your physique and has a possitive effect on your cardiovascular system.
